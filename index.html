  Welcome  

For nearly 10 years, Maoxu Li has been studying and working in the ever-changing field of software development after received his Bachelor's degree in communication engineering from Northern Jiaotong University in China in 1997. With extensive experience in programming, software design, and team management, He has built up strong professional skills and a deep understanding of the requirements and solutions of digital video surveillance system and peer-to-peer (P2P) video streaming system.

His early experience focused on developing a digital video surveillance system. DVR (Digital Video Recorder) and NVR (Network Video Recorder) are key applications. In the latest three years, he led teams in developing a Peer-to-Peer (P2P) video streaming system.

Not only does he have the design engineering and technical skills to succeed as a software engineer, he also communicate well with diverse types of people. It is his duty to provide trade shows support for his products and solutions to the key customers.
