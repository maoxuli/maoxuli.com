About me

I am a son, a brother, a husband, and going to be a father in September 2012. Meanwhile I am a software engineer. In general I am a versatile man. I like and I am good at cooking, gardening, and repairing of anything in home or out of home. At work I was a recognized c++ programmer and a good team leader too.

I was born in Northeast China and completed elementary school, middle school, and high school there. My undergraduate study was completed in 1997 in Beijing, China. My family, including my parents and sister, moved to Beijing later when I worked in Beijing and got married there. Those were so wonderful days.

In May 2009, I came to the United States with my wife. She earned a Visiting Fellow award from NIH for research training at NCI. I want to make a change to my life. My plan is learning about America and taking some graduate courses at college if possible. I did it. I graduated from The George Washington University in May 2011 and received a Master of Science degree in Computer Science.
