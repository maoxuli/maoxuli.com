GEV/DVx: A software framework for digital video applications

Jan 2005 - May 2006, Golden Vision Technology Co. Ltd., Beijing China

In early 2005, my employer Golden Eye Technology was reorganized as Golden Vision Technology. I was entitled as a Project Manager and Software Architect to lead developing a software framework for digital video applications. The design and implementation is based on reconstructing and refactoring of a existing digital video surveillance system (GoldenEye).

About the system

Safeguarding system was expected to be a part of enterprise information architecture. Current digital video surveillance systems were not easy to integrate with management information system (MIS). GEV/DVx tried to construct a new software framework for digital video applications, in which the devices, data, work flows, work log, even states are treated as resources under the information architecture. To be simple, GEV/DVx was designed to support the networking, intelligence, and informatization of safeguarding system based on digital video surveillance.

Functionally, GEV/DVx consists of two parts. Application management information system (MIS) and real-time digital video processing system. The management of hardware and software, users, data, log, and states are based on typical MIS architecture, which make it easy to integrate with other applications. Digital video processing system is a DirectShow-like framework, which concentrates on media-related work, including video capture and display, record and transfer, state control and actions, audio and video detection and analysis. Functions are wrapped into filters that can be configured to connect to each other to complete requirement functions.
