PPEngine - Could Internet be Peer-to-Peer?

Computer Science is a discipline with very strong practicality. Software design and programming is a necessary part of the coursework. I chose Peer-to-Peer (P2P) as the topic and arranged all class projects around a single software development project. This is the origination of PPEngine.

The aim of PPEngine is to explore the possibility of future Internet that works in a Peer-to-Peer fashion. [Details...]
