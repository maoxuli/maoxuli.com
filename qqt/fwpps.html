[ QQ Technology | FireWind/PPStream ]

FireWind/PPStream: A peer-to-peer video streaming system

Jun 2006 - Dec 2007, QQ Technology Inc., Beijing China

FireWind/PPStream is a peer-to-peer networking system dedicated to Internet video streaming applications with low-latency. It supports video broadcast (TV) and video-on-demand (VOD) within a single system architecture.
